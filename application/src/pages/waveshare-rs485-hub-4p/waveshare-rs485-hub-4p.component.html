Module Notes

This is an industrial rail-mount RS485 hub that provides 4-ch RS485 slave ports through 1-ch RS485 master port. In the fireCNC system, it acts as a signal repeater and amplifier, extending the communication distance and allowing more slave devices (like servo motors) to be connected to the RS485 bus reliably.

Key Features

  • Signal Isolation: Provides photoelectric isolation for the master and slave ports to suppress interference.
  • Power Isolation: Features un-isolated power for the master port and isolated power for the slave ports.
  • Protection: Onboard TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) for lightning protection and anti-surge, plus resettable fuses and protection diodes for current/voltage stability.
  • Wide Voltage Range: Supports a power supply range of 9 ~ 24V DC.
  • Casing: Industrial rail-mount ABS case for easy installation and durability.
